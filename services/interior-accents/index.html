Interior accents

How we approach interior accents

Perfect for homeowners who want craftsmanship-forward details paired with outdoor and exterior projects.

We're careful with dust control and finish carpentry so accent work feels intentional, not patched in. You'll see the same attention to detail you expect from our exterior builds—tight joints, level lines, and materials chosen to age well.

Where we focus

Beams & ceiling detail

Box beams, faux timbers, and ceiling accents laid out for sightlines and lighting—integrated so they read as part of the architecture.

Mantels & surrounds

Custom profiles, depths, and materials scaled to your fireplace and room, with clean returns and hearth transitions.

Built-ins & storage

Closets, mudrooms, bars, and shelving built for daily use—layout, adjustable shelving, and hardware that feels solid.

Stain, paint & handoff

Finish schedules coordinated with your timeline so trim, beams, and built-ins are protected and touch-up ready when we walk the job with you.

What to expect

  • Beams: Faux or structural aesthetics installed with clean joints and ceiling integration.
  • Mantels: Custom profiles and materials sized to your fireplace and room scale.
  • Closets: Layout, shelving, and hardware for daily usability.

Common projects we take on

  • Great-room and kitchen ceiling beam packages
  • Custom fireplace mantels and surround upgrades
  • Walk-in closets, mudrooms, and pantry built-ins
  • Accent shelving, bars, and niche trim details
