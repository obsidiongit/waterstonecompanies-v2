# Homepage Layout Blueprint

Based on the analysis of the Archadeck of Northern Colorado website, here is the proposed hierarchy and layout for the new Waterstone Companies homepage. This flow transitions seamlessly from inspirational visuals down to professional processes and social proof.

## Visual Flow Breakdown

1.  **Promo Bar:** A thin bar at the top with a seasonal call-to-action (e.g., "Start Planning Your Summer Oasis Today").
2.  **Header:**
    *   **Logo:** Left-aligned Waterstone Companies logo.
    *   **Utility Nav (Top Right):** Phone Number, Service Area indicator.
    *   **Main Nav (Below):** "Services", "Portfolio", "Our Process", "Resources", and a prominent "Get Started" or "Consultation" button.
3.  **Hero Section:**
    *   Full-width high-quality image or video background of a premium outdoor living space.
    *   **Headline:** Strong value proposition (e.g., "Premier Specialty Outdoor Living Contractor").
    *   **Subheadline:** Bullet points of key services.
    *   **Primary CTA:** Large lead capture button ("REQUEST FREE ESTIMATE").
4.  **Process Section:**
    *   A three-column layout breaking down the service journey: **Consult**, **Design**, and **Build**.
    *   Icon/image paired with descriptive text for each step.
5.  **Gallery/Services Section:**
    *   A dynamic grid of project categories (e.g., Decks, Patios, Hardscapes, Fire Features).
    *   Designed with large, engaging hover-effect images to drive deeper navigation.
6.  **Local Introduction (Trust & SEO):**
    *   Text focusing on specialized expertise in the target service areas.
    *   Video thumbnail with a play button to introduce the team/owner.
7.  **Interaction/Quick Links Cards:**
    *   Large, tappable cards guiding users to specific intents: **Inspiration**, **Why Us**, **Our Process**, **Get Started**.
8.  **About Section:**
    *   A brief write-up emphasizing local ownership, contracting expertise, and quality guarantees.
9.  **Lead Magnet:**
    *   A promotional block offering a downloadable "Outdoor Living Design Guide" in exchange for email capture.
10. **Awards & Partners Slider:**
    *   Scrolling logos of trusted brands (e.g., Trex, TimberTech) and certifications.
11. **Testimonials:**
    *   A carousel of client reviews, ideally with a "Google Reviews" badge and rating.
12. **Service Areas:**
    *   A cleanly formatted list or map indicating all operating regions.
13. **Footer:**
    *   Comprehensive site map links, social media icons, office address, and copyright information.
